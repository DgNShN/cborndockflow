# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We expect everyone contributing to cborn DocFlow to help keep the project welcoming, respectful, and productive.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on the code, the issue, or the idea.
- Keep feedback specific and actionable.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or abusive language.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or reviews.
- Sharing private or sensitive information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation if behavior harms the project or its contributors.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and any project-related communication.
